Identify and eliminate product defects using computer vision and AI

Our solution can be used to monitor the quality of every product that arrives on the production line. Based on the data we collect from our partners, our AI & Computer Vision based object detectors & classifiers can segment out relevant parts, and check if they are defective or not.

We offer these as API-based solutions for both images & realtime videos. Our primary intent is to:

  • Reduce fixing and remediation cost by early detection of defects
  • Track each product and the stages where defects are maximum to optimse production

Localize & classify the type of defects in manufactured parts

Along with detection of defects, we also provide solutions for our partners to understand the manufacturing processes deeper and automatically classify the various kinds of defects in their products. Once our AI models identify the type of defects, we can also generate basic analytics about these defects.

Some key aspects of our defect categorization services include:

  • Accurate results to identify problematic process in entire production
  • Using transfer learning to differentiate between different types of defects that appear visually similar.
  • Assign importance of defect based on severity of the defect to quickly identify the particular category of defect

In future, we also plan to integrate identification of custom precautionary & remediation measures based on the types & frequency of defects observed.
